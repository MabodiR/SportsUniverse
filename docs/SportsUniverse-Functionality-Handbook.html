1 · Foundation

Platform overview and roles

SportsUniverse is a sports-first social, talent, opportunity and commercial platform. The Laravel backend serves the Inertia/Vue web application and versioned APIs used by web and mobile clients.

Supported account roles

AthleteFanCoachRefereeLinesmanScoutAgentClubAcademyBusinessSponsorAdminSystem adminSuper admin

Responsive delivery

The web interface is responsive for desktop and mobile browsers. Versioned APIs provide the same core data and business rules for a mobile client, including authentication, feeds, profiles, messaging, notifications and media.

4 · Core experience

Feed and recommendation engine

For You

The For You feed selects published, public, approved sports content. It uses precomputed recommendation IDs when available and falls back to candidate retrieval and live ranking. Ranking considers interests, content metadata, engagement and freshness; diversification reduces repetition. Fan sport preferences, location, sport and position can narrow candidates.

Following

The Following feed contains normal posts from followed accounts and the viewer’s own posts, ordered newest first. Stories are separated into the Stories row instead of being repeated as ordinary feed cards.

Scrolling and repetition

  • Cursor pagination loads a small page at a time; it never retrieves the full database.
  • Client-side duplicate keys prevent repeated items on the same scrolling session.
  • Not-interested preferences remove a post, creator, sport or similar topic from future candidate sets.
  • Impression, skip, replay, watch duration and completion signals improve subsequent recommendations.
Eligible candidates→Preferences and safety→Rank→Diversify→Sponsored placement→Cursor page
Scale design: feed endpoints operate on indexed candidate subsets and cursor pages. The five-million-post dataset is intended for performance testing, not bulk delivery to the browser.
5 · Publishing

Posts, uploads and media

Supported publishing formats

  • MP4, MOV and WebM video.
  • JPG, PNG and WebP images.
  • Up to ten pictures in a carousel, with a selectable cover.
  • A combined video-and-picture carousel where supplied.

Creator workflow

Select or capture→Trim and improve→Add sports metadata→Upload→Process and moderate→Publish
EditorCurrent behavior
Video trimSelect start point and a post length up to 60 seconds. Story videos are capped at 30 seconds.
Visual adjustmentsBrightness, contrast, colour saturation, rotation, output width and compression quality.
Sports metadataSport, country, league, team, competition, content type, language, skills/topics and location.
AudienceEveryone, followers or only the creator for normal posts. Stories are follower-only unless promoted.

Creators can edit captions, hashtags, location, cover and visibility; publish drafts; list their posts, likes, favourites and reposts; or delete content they own.

6 · Ephemeral content

Stories

  • A Story can contain video or pictures.
  • Video length cannot exceed 30 seconds; the limit is enforced in the editor, API and background finalisation.
  • The lifetime begins when processing completes and the Story becomes published.
  • Every Story expires exactly 24 hours after publication.
  • Organic Stories are visible to the creator and followers only.

Placement

SurfaceStory behavior
For YouShows “Your Story” for creating or viewing the creator’s own active Story. Organic follower Stories do not appear as regular For You posts.
FollowingShows the dedicated horizontal Story circles for followed creators. Organic Stories are not duplicated in the normal Following post list.
Promoted placementAn approved, paid Story promotion can enter the normal For You feed for matching non-followers, but promotion never extends the 24-hour expiry.

Viewer

Tapping a circle opens a full-screen viewer. Images progress after six seconds; videos progress when playback ends. Viewers can move backward or forward, close the viewer, see remaining lifetime and use a sponsored call to action.

8 · Find people and content

Discovery and search

  • Unified search across people and posts.
  • Search by term, creator, hashtag, sport and location metadata.
  • Saved searches and search-history clearing.
  • Saved profiles.
  • Women in Sports hub for profiles, stories and opportunities.
  • Recommended and trending accounts based on recent views and weighted engagement.
  • Feed links for sport, position and location.

Content intelligence

Posts store structured sport, country, league, team, competition, content type, language, skills, detected labels and topic weights. These features distinguish related areas—for example English football from South African football—rather than treating every football post as identical.

9 · Communication

Messaging and notifications

Messaging

A first message to a non-mutual connection is sent as a request. Requests can be accepted or declined; accepted relationships open conversations. Conversations support read state, archive/unarchive, mute, typing state, reporting, editing/deleting owned messages and blocking.

Notifications

Notifications are grouped by date and category, include unread counts and allow individual deletion or “mark all read.” Preference controls cover messages, requests, opportunities, followers, engagement, moderation, profile views and email digest.

  • Database notifications are the durable source.
  • Realtime events update the open interface.
  • Expo push jobs support mobile notifications when configured.
  • Moderation notifications include AI flags, review results and appeal actions.

13 · Commercial growth

Advertising and sponsorship

Campaign types

TypeGoals
Post or Story promotionViews, followers, website visits or awareness.
Sponsorship campaignApplications, website visits or awareness without requiring an existing post.

Creation flow

Preview post→Choose goal→Audience and budget→Review→PayFast→Admin review

The post selector displays video or image previews before selection. Targeting supports sport, gender, province and age range. Daily budget and duration generate estimated reach and a total.

Delivery controls

  • Only active, paid and approved campaigns are eligible.
  • Campaign dates, remaining budget, daily pacing and per-viewer frequency caps are enforced.
  • Blocked users and not-interested preferences remain excluded.
  • Audience metadata is matched before insertion.
  • A promoted post is not duplicated on the same page.
  • Impressions, clicks, video views, profile visits, follows, reach and spend are tracked by delivery.

PayFast

Campaign and membership checkouts generate signed browser POST forms. Return, cancellation and ITN routes are separate. ITNs validate source, signature, merchant, amount and PayFast server confirmation before changing payment or campaign state.

Sandbox rule: PayFast default sandbox Merchant ID 10000100 and Key 46f0cd694581a must use an empty passphrase. Live mode requires the exact live account credentials and matching passphrase.

16 · Trust and relevance

Moderation and appeals

General moderation

  • Users can report supported content with a reason and details.
  • Admins review open reports, pending/flagged media and flagged posts.
  • Actions can approve, flag, hide, reject or restore according to the content type.
  • Every administrator decision is written to a moderation audit log.

Sports-relevance vision engine

A separate Python service samples eight frames across a video or reviews up to ten post images. A CLIP vision model compares the visuals with sports and non-sports concepts, then returns a sports-relevance score and explanation.

Sample visuals→Vision score→Threshold→Keep or flag→Human decision
  • Low-scoring published content becomes flagged and leaves public feeds.
  • The recommendation is “review for removal”; the engine never permanently deletes content.
  • Admins see all AI suggestions, score, reason, creator and media.

Creator appeal

The creator receives a moderation notification and can send a message explaining why the content is sports-related. Duplicate pending appeals are blocked. Admins see the appeal alongside the AI evidence and can restore the post or uphold removal. The result is sent back to the creator and audited.

19 · Scale

Data scale and production operations

Large feed dataset

The mass-feed generator can create up to five million deterministic PostgreSQL posts across configured sports, reusing licensed sports images and sampled source videos. It inserts in batches, creates topic records and resumes based on deterministic IDs.

  • Command: php artisan feed:seed-mass-posts --count=5000000 --batch=100000.
  • The operation is idempotent for the deterministic mass-feed ID range.
  • The dedicated automatic production workflow was intentionally removed; production import can be handled through DBeaver as requested.
